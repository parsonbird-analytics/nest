About Parsonbird Analytics

With over 8 years of experience in data analysis and performance management, I've helped businesses transform complex data into clear, actionable insights. The name Parsonbird comes from the English name for New Zealand's tui - reflecting my English heritage and love for my adopted home. Just as the tui sees clearly from great heights, I help businesses gain clear perspective on their performance data.
